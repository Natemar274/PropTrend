About PropTrend

PropTrend is a small project aiming to make macro-level Australian real estate data more easily accessible. Source data is drawn mainly from the Australian Bureau of Statistics (ABS), and a few other publicly available sources, interpolated where necessary, with minor transformations applied to produce a continuous dataset up to the present.

Forecasts five years into the future are generated using simple exponential smoothing — a time series method that applies exponentially decreasing weights to past observations to forecast future values. It assumes recent data is more indicative of future trends, without overfitting.

The idea arose from the difficulty of finding high-level, clean, and publicly accessible property market data presented in a usable format.

This site is under development. More tools and data visualisations are coming soon.
